## 7. Acceptance Criteria

The reporting output is acceptable when:

- [ ] source data route is documented;
- [ ] KPI definitions are agreed;
- [ ] quality checks are defined;
- [ ] output answers the stated decision question;
- [ ] caveats and limitations are visible;
- [ ] owner can refresh or hand over the report;
- [ ] review forum knows how to record actions.
